The Problem

Decisions without data are just guesses

Most SMEs have data, in WhatsApp messages, spreadsheets, receipt books, accounting apps, but no way to make sense of it together. So decisions are made on gut feel. We take what you already have, clean it, and build reports that make the right decision obvious.

The Reality

What running on gut feel actually costs

Most SME owners know their business is doing well or poorly, but they can't say exactly why. Which product has the best margin? Which customer segment generates the most repeat business? Which month is always slow and why? Without answers to these, decisions default to instinct. Sometimes instinct is right. Often it's expensive.

Data doesn't remove judgement. It gives it a foundation. The goal isn't to turn your business into a spreadsheet. It's to stop making ₦100,000 decisions with ₦0 of information.

Restocking what doesn't sell

Without visibility into which products or services move, cash gets tied up in dead stock or wasted effort. A simple sales report would show the pattern in minutes.

Marketing to the wrong people

Spending on ads or promotions without knowing which customer type actually converts is common, and costly. The data usually shows the answer was there all along.

Not seeing a cash drain until it's too late

Expenses creeping up, margins quietly shrinking, one service subsidising another. These are invisible on a busy month but obvious in a proper monthly report.
