Night Eye

Kengalia Art — 50 pieces

An owl sees in darkness. These fifty pieces were built image by image: each one a different read on the same creature. Bold colour. Precise form. The kind of quiet that surrounds something watching you back.

Night Eye — Kengalia Art

Each work in this series was made individually. No templates. No repeats. The owl reappears in each piece but never the same way twice: different colour palette, different composition, different weight on the eye.

“Owls see what others miss.”

Digital medium

All fifty works are original digital art, built entirely by hand using mobile digital tools. No AI generation. No filters applied to photographs. Every colour choice and line was placed deliberately.
