About

Godspower is an Information Systems Specialist who has spent years at the intersection of business and technology — helping organisations understand their digital infrastructure and use it better. He has worked in Nigeria and internationally, across industries ranging from pharmaceuticals to hospitality.

He founded Kengalia because he saw a gap: professional digital services were hard to access and harder to trust in the Nigerian market. He built the business he wished existed — one that brings the same standards to African clients that they would receive anywhere else in the world.

Outside of client work, he is building Perfcent, expanding the Kengalia ecosystem, and pursuing his own creative practice through art and design.
